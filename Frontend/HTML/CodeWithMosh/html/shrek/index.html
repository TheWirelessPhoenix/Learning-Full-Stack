🧅 My Epic Shrek 🧅

Shrek is love. Shrek is life. Shrek is layers.

"WHAT ARE YOU DOING IN MY SWAMP?!"

Behold! Shrek — a magnificent, swamp-dwelling ogre with the emotional depth of an onion and the personal hygiene of a garbage truck. He lives in a swamp, eats rats for breakfast, and has a talking donkey as his best friend (which says more about him than he'd like to admit). Despite smelling like a bog and scaring villagers for sport, Shrek has somehow managed to find true love, become a father, and save the kingdom of Far Far Away — all while wearing the same tunic for what appears to be several decades. He's proof that with enough confidence (and a Scottish accent), even a flatulent ogre can become a hero. In short: Shrek is love. Shrek is life. Shrek is an absolute icon.
